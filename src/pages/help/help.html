This app is designed to be used to calculate the active insulin on board.

Input your current BG reading, how many carbs you are eating if any, how many hours since your last bolus, and your sensitvity ratio (as 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, or 80)

Possible error warnings: Enter your senesitivity ratio percentage only. Enter your bg only if in range 150-450.
